Welcome to the Salmon Blog

Salmon Team Feb 20, 2026 2 min read

We're excited to launch the Salmon blog.

This is where we'll share what we're learning as we build the real-time data engine that keeps CRM records accurate, current, and trusted. Expect perspectives on the problems that keep revenue teams up at night — and how better data infrastructure can solve them.

What we'll cover

Here's what you can expect to find here:

  • CRM data quality — Why your contact records decay, what it costs you, and how to fix it at the source rather than the symptom.
  • Revenue operations — Practical approaches to enrichment, routing, and scoring that actually move pipeline.
  • Compliance & KYC — How real-time verification changes the game for regulated industries.
  • Product updates — New capabilities, integrations, and under-the-hood improvements to the Salmon engine.
  • Industry analysis — Our take on where B2B data infrastructure is heading.

Why a blog?

We talk to revenue teams every day. The same questions come up again and again: How do I know which records are stale? What's the real cost of bad data? How do I build a data stack that doesn't rot?

We want to answer those questions in the open — not behind a gated PDF, not in a sales deck. Just clear, honest writing about the problems we care about.

Stay in the loop

You can subscribe via RSS or just bookmark this page. We'll keep things concise and worth your time.

If there's a topic you'd like us to dig into, reach out at hello@salmonrun.ai. We'd love to hear from you.
