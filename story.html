Our Story

We built Salmon with one goal — deliver clean, useful data instantly.

Enterprise data shouldn't be static, stale, or stitched together from five different vendors. We started Salmon to fix that.

Our Philosophy

We believe data should be living.

We've scaled growth teams. We've run data ops. We know the reality: millions wasted on static lists, outreach campaigns built on half-truths, hours lost hunting for updated contacts that should have been verified the moment you needed them.

Companies spend billions on sales and marketing intelligence — and 40% of it goes stale before the next quarter even begins. Roles change. Emails bounce. Fraud slips through. The best teams fix it manually or patch it with half-broken point solutions.

Your CRM should clean itself. Our AI agents source, verify and enrich records continuously — so your team never wastes time on stale leads, ghost contacts, or broken campaigns.

Our Promise

No more stale data. Ever.

Salmon is the real-time data engine that plugs into your CRM and keeps it alive. Our AI agents source, verify, and enrich records continuously — so your team never wastes time on stale leads, ghost contacts, or broken campaigns.

Today, we power sales and marketing orgs with live prospect data. Next, we're bringing real-time intelligence to fraud, compliance, and hiring. Wherever trust and timing matter, your data should keep up — automatically.

It doesn't have to be this way. We believe your systems should verify themselves. Your CRM and pipeline should stay clean on its own. Data should be a living, dynamic layer — always up-to-date, always accurate, always ready to move when you do.

No more static vendors. No more stale CSVs. No more guesswork. Just data that works as hard as you do — and never goes stale.
