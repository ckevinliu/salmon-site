Our Process

From kickoff to always-on data

We work alongside your team, not in a black box. Here's how a typical engagement unfolds.

1
1–2 Weeks

Discovery & Kickoff

We begin with a discovery call to understand what's slowing your revenue teams down. Common issues include stale contacts, incomplete accounts, and patchy CRM data. We build requirements, run a proof-of-concept data test, and align on scope based on business priorities.

2
2–4 Weeks

Test, Refine, Scale

Your pilot results shape the next move. We refine your ICP, run iterative testing, align on a working model, then expand. Whether you need one iteration or a few, we're in it with you — ready to plug straight into Salesforce, HubSpot, or your preferred stack.

3
Ongoing

Long-Term Partnership

Once we're live, we don't disappear. Expect data maintenance, refresh cycles (monthly, quarterly, etc.), and dedicated support. For larger teams, you'll have a named engineer and priority support. Need API integration? We'll partner with your devs, side-by-side.
