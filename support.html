Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Salmon different from ZoomInfo, Clearbit, or Clay?

+
Unlike static databases or rigid enrichment tools, Salmon delivers real-time data that adapts to your exact fields. No pre-set schemas, no stale records. Book a demo to see the difference.

How accurate is the data?

+
Every data point is verified across multiple sources and passed through an AI + human QA layer when needed. The result: higher precision and fewer errors compared to legacy vendors. Start a pilot and compare side by side.

Where does your data come from?

+
Salmon combines three source types: open web data, partnered/paywalled datasets, and your own customer-owned data. This ensures broad coverage, depth, and compliance without sacrificing security.

How fast can we get started?

+
Most teams launch a pilot within a day and go live in just a few weeks. No six-month IT project required. Let's scope an engagement together.

Can Salmon work with my CRM or data warehouse?

+
Yes. Salmon integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, Snowflake, and others — plus we offer secure APIs for custom setups. Tell us your stack and we'll handle the rest.

Do you support custom fields?

+
Absolutely. Salmon is built to be schema-flexible. Any field, any format — enriched and verified in real time.

Is Salmon secure and compliant?

+
Yes. We're built for enterprise-grade security standards. Customer-owned data is never shared or commingled. Learn about our security practices.

How does pricing work?

+
Our model is usage-based — you only pay for the enrichments you need, with enterprise plans available for scale. Most customers start with a pilot to prove value. See pricing.
