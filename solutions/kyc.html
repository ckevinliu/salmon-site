The real problem

Every day of KYC friction is a day a legitimate customer waits to give you money.

Every day your KYC process takes is a day a legitimate customer is waiting to give you money. Legacy verification tools were built for a world with weekly batch jobs and compliance teams who had time to review manually. That world is gone.

The modern compliance challenge is three-pronged: speed (applicants expect near-instant decisions), accuracy (fraud is more sophisticated and synthetic identities are harder to detect), and continuity (a clean onboarding doesn't mean a clean customer in 18 months). Point-in-time verification solves one third of the problem.

Salmon's AI engine handles all three. Real-time verification at point of application. Multi-source triangulation that catches what single-source APIs miss. And continuous post-onboarding monitoring that flags changes to your approved customer base before they become your compliance team's problem.
