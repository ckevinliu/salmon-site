The real problem

The problem isn't dirty data. It's data that gets dirty faster than you can clean it.

The standard playbook — buy a list, import it, run a quarterly clean — was designed for a world where people stayed in their jobs for five years. The average B2B professional now changes roles every 18–24 months. By the time your quarterly refresh runs, 10–15% of your database has moved.

Most RevOps teams are spending 2–3 hours per week on manual verification tasks that should take seconds. Your ops people are doing busywork your CRM vendor should handle automatically. They're not.

Salmon treats your CRM as a living system, not a static file. Salmon monitors every record for changes — role shifts, company pivots, contact departures, tech stack changes — and updates your database in real time. Your team opens Salesforce and the data is accurate. That's the entire job.
